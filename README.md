Celestial Services is a feature-rich Discord bot built for fun, economy, and games. It supports both / slash commands and - prefix commands.

💰 Economy System
Earn, spend, and compete for coins. Claim your daily reward, gamble it away, steal from others, or send coins to friends. A live leaderboard tracks the richest members on the server.

🎮 Casino & Games
Seven games to test your luck:

🎰 Slots — spin the reels for up to 10× your bet
🃏 Blackjack — play against the dealer with real Hit/Stand buttons
🎡 Roulette — bet on red, black, or green (17:1)
✂️ Rock Paper Scissors — go head-to-head with the bot
🎲 Dice Duel — roll higher than the bot to win
🪙 Coin Flip — call heads or tails
🧠 Trivia — answer questions to earn free coins
🎯 Word Game
A live channel-wide guessing game where letters are revealed over time. Buy hints with coins and race to guess the word before it's too late.

🔧 Utilities
Ping, server info, user info, polls, and a clean /help menu.

Every win and loss comes with a unique message — from "🚀 Private jet unlocked" to "🦈 You are trapped on Epstein Island."
